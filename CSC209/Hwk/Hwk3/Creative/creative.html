  • Any Pasta (best with penne pasta)
  • Vodka
  • Tomato Paste
  • Heavy Cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Oregano
  • Basil Leaves
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning

Instructions:

                1. Boil pasta of choice until soft
                
                
                2. Drain pasta, and put to side
                3. In a pan add butter and tomato paste
                4. Add pepper
                5. Add salt
                6. Add cayene pepper if you would like
                7. Add oregano and italian seasoning
                8. Add garlic and onion powder
                9. Add heavy cream and basil leaves and stir until creamy
                10. Add vodka and stir for 5min in low heat
                11. Finally, add pasta and stir until evenly coated
